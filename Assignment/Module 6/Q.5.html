Q.5 What are different types of lists in HTML?

  • Ordered Lists:
    Ordered lists are used when the list items need to be displayed in a specific order.

    Example:

    1. Item 1
    2. Item 2

  • Unordered Lists:
    Unordered lists are used when the order of the list items does not matter.

    Example:

    • Item 1
    • Item 2
    • Item 3

  • Description List:

    • Coffee
    - black hot drink
    Milk
    - white cold drink
